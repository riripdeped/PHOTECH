About PhoTech

About Us

PhoTech is more than just a photo printing service; we're a student-run business corporation dedicated to blending creativity with innovation. As part of our Entrepreneurship and Empowerment Technology project at Don Bosco Senior High School Paranaque, we aim to provide affordable, convenient, and high-quality photo printing services to the school community. We turn digital photos into lasting physical prints using custom-designed templates and premium materials.

Our Vision

We envision a school community where photo printing is convenient, accessible, and affordable for every student‚Äîwithout the need to travel far or wait in long lines.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fast, affordable, and high-quality photo printing services tailored specifically for students. We aim to reduce the stress of errands by bringing printing closer to home or campus, using available resources and student-friendly prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to print a photo?

Our prices start at just ‚Ç±25 per print, depending on size and template. Check our Promos page for bundle discounts!

How long does it take to get my prints?

Most orders are ready for pickup the next school day. During busy periods, we'll notify you if there are any delays.

Where do I pick up my order?

You can pick up your prints at Room 304 in the Senior High School building during break times or after school.

We accept cash upon pickup or GCash for advance payments. Receipts are provided for all transactions.
